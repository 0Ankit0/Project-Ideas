# Settlement and Incident Resolution

## Failure Mode
Incident charge is posted but compensation credit is not applied after approved dispute outcome.

## Impact
Incorrect balances, compliance risk, and prolonged case closure times.

## Detection
Settlement integrity report compares incident decision outcomes against resulting ledger movements.

## Recovery / Mitigation
Apply compensating entries, require dual-approval for manual adjustments, and close case only after balance parity checks pass.
