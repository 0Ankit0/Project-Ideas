# Checkout, Check-in, and Condition Disputes

## Failure Mode
Return condition evidence is incomplete, causing disagreement on damage responsibility and deposit handling.

## Impact
Settlement delays, dispute escalation, and trust erosion between participants.

## Detection
Inspection workflow alerts on missing pre/post condition artifacts or mismatched assessor signatures.

## Recovery / Mitigation
Enforce mandatory media capture checklist, second-review workflow, and structured adjudication timeline with audit trail.
