# Business Rules - E-Commerce

## Pricing & Promotions
- Discounts cannot exceed 70% without admin approval.
- Only one coupon can be applied per order unless explicitly allowed.

## Inventory
- Orders cannot be confirmed if available quantity is insufficient.
- Inventory is reserved for 15 minutes after checkout begins.

## Payments
- Capture must occur within 7 days of authorization.
- Refunds are initiated within 24 hours of return approval.

## Shipping
- Orders above the free-shipping threshold are auto-upgraded.
- High-value items require signature on delivery.

## Returns
- Returns allowed within 30 days for eligible categories.
- Opened consumables are non-returnable.
