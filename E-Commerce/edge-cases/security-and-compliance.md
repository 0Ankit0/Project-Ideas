# Security and Compliance Edge Cases

## Failure Mode
Credential stuffing campaign triggers high-volume login attempts during flash sale window.

## Impact
Account takeover risk, fraud exposure, and degraded availability.

## Detection
Anomaly rules detect abnormal failed-login velocity and suspicious geo/device shifts.

## Recovery / Mitigation
Enable adaptive MFA, risk-based step-up checks, temporary throttling, and forced session/token rotation for affected users.
